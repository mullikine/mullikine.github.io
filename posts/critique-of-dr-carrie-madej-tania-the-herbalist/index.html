Critique of Dr Carrie Madej & Tania the Herbalist

Jan 25, 2021
5 min read
covidantivaxnonsense

Here is my personal critique after listening to this video

Original article
Dr Carrie Madej & Tania the Herbalist - YouTube

Claims

Part of us can be owned by whoever owns the patent of the covid vaccine

Ridiculous.

The claim is that if it’s actually taken up into the genome and it’s permanent then part of us can be owned by the patent.

This is lunacy and very unrealistic that this would ever occur.

hydrogel – the way of hooking us up to a smart device

Ridiculous.

It’s very unlikely to be the way. Far more likely is simply using computer vision or neuralink.

The 060606 patent is very unlikely to have an impact

2019 is quite late anyway.

I started investing in streamr in 2017, 2 years before the patent.

The patent has no power to take ownership of already established cryptocurrencies that are owned by the public (ethereum / streamr etc.).

that’s why we need 5G or 6G for all of that data to be collected in the cloud

That’s not the reason for 5G/6G.

More generally, fast and pervasive data transmission is the reason for 5G or 6G.

Claiming that the reason is for data to be taken from the human body is like saying the reason the internet was invented was to have online access to the black market.

It has nothing to do specifically with gathering data from the human body, though it’s a viable use case.

you will be scanned like a product

Ridiculous.

She claims that cryptocurrency is stored inside the human body but it is not.

If you’re a good human you get more crypto and if you’re a bad human you get less

Ridiculous.

Morals are not encoded into cryptocurrency and cryptocurrency is resilient to manipulation and is subject to the consensus of its users.

Holders of cryptocurrency in general value privacy and personal freedom.

In fact, cryptocurrency is trying to debase the establishment.

mentioned

hydrogel

This seems to be pandemic-related panic.

Neuralink is not to do with hydrogel.

Using the matrix and elon musk

luciferase

Nothing to do with COVID.

It has applications in genetic research generally and was discovered in 1885.

DARPA

There are things which have not come into the mainstream because they are not commercially ready / cheap enough, technology not ready.

This is why Elon Musk was able to invent digital money exchange, achieve cheap space flight, make the electric car successful.

DARPA has done research on such things but it wont reach mainstream until the private sector arrives there.

vision of the presenter

The person talking has depicted cryptocurrencies as being a tool used to gain information about a person and control them.

refutation

A person doesn’t need to share their data, but if they do they can be rewarded fairly for it.

A cryptocurrency is only valuable because people want it to succeed.

The cryptocurrencies that are started by corporations (example, Hedera) are unlikely to displace currencies such as ethereum because people do not trust them.

https://hedera.com/

The crypto movement is a force for good.

A better vision of the future

Cryptocurrencies would enable society to retain its structure going into the future.

They do this by allowing people to come to a concensus on things for which future infrastructure can then be based on.

Examples of applications of cryptocurrency

  • A dynamically evolving consensus on the meaning of words and phrases in the English language.
    • There would then be an objective thing, like a dictionary which you can study optimally.
  • A dynamically evolving consensus on the genetic code of a species, such as what it means genetically to be a human being.
    • On top of such a cryptocurrency, you could base applications for growing organs and societies where there is regulation, debate and trust.
    • Without the cryptocurrency there would be unregulated automation and experimentation.
  • A cryptocurrency Creating jobs for people based on services and allowing them to retain part of their dignity
  • Creating businesses based on services that

Cryptocurrencies may represent any resource and as the ethereum network grows, new types of resources will appear, which were previously intangible.

For example, if a teacher chooses to live in an area where teaches are sorely needed, they may generate money just by being in proximity.

The point of cryptocurrencies is they increase in value the more people trust in them. It means you can trust that when you pay for something you get the resource that it represents, or that the supply chain has been carried out without any shortcuts.

Critique

They are simply talking about the real technology in a bad light.

Agendas posited

  • They are patenting everything.

    Patenting cryptocurrency is a ridiculous concept because cryptocurrencies generally are investments which are owned and governed by the users and not subject to manipulation from anyone other than the whales that own vast portions of it.

    At the most fundamental level they represent a distrust of the establishment.

    A complete takeover of the crypto market by the establishment is increasingly unlikely as crypto increases in value and existing currencies gain adoption – it becomes increasingly unattainable for everyone, including governments.
